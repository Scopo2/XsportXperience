Purpose

The purpose of this Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) is to establish guidelines for the acceptable use of information technology resources within EsportXperience to protect all users, the organization, and its assets.

Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party users who access EsportXperience's information systems, networks, and resources.

Acceptable Use

  1. Authorized Access: Users must only access systems and data for which they have explicit permission. Sharing of login credentials is strictly prohibited.
  2. Professional Conduct: Users are expected to communicate respectfully and professionally while using company resources, including emails, chats, and other communication tools.
  3. Compliance with Laws: Users must comply with all applicable local, state, and federal laws regarding data protection, intellectual property, and privacy.
  4. Data Protection: Sensitive and confidential information must be handled with care. Users must follow company guidelines for data encryption, storage, and sharing.
  5. Resource Usage: Use of company resources should be primarily for work-related purposes. Limited personal use is allowed as long as it does not interfere with job performance or violate this policy.
  6. Software Installation: Installation of unauthorized software or applications on company devices is prohibited. All software must be approved by the IT department.

Prohibited Use

  1. Malicious Activities: Engaging in activities that could harm the companyâ€™s systems or data, including hacking, introducing malware, or phishing, is strictly forbidden.
  2. Inappropriate Content: Users must not access, distribute, or store content that is illegal, offensive, or discriminatory, including but not limited to hate speech, pornography, or harassment.
  3. Excessive Personal Use: Excessive use of company resources for personal activities, such as streaming media or gaming, is prohibited if it negatively impacts productivity or bandwidth.
  4. Bypassing Security Controls: Attempting to disable or circumvent security measures, including firewalls and antivirus software, is strictly prohibited.

Consequences of Violating the Policy

Violations of this Acceptable Use Policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract, legal action, and financial liability for damages caused.

Policy Review and Modifications

This policy may be reviewed and modified at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes to the policy.

Acknowledgment

By using EsportXperience's information resources, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with this Acceptable Use Policy.
